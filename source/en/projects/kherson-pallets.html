People continue to live in Ukrainian cities close to the front line. Kherson and Kupyansk were on the brink of a humanitarian disaster. Currently, thousands of people are experiencing unbearable hardship due to the devastating impact of conflict, natural disasters and socio-economic difficulties.

Every day the situation becomes more and more critical, and now our main priority is to provide urgent humanitarian aid to those who need it most. Together we can change the lives of many people and support them in difficult times.
