What has been done in accordance with the requirements

Thanks to the work of the foundation and with the help of our partners, the military unit A5617 has been provided with medicines in excess of UAH 240,000. We delivered compression systems, bandages, analgesic and antipyretic drugs, elastic bandages, etc.

Our team is willing to create projects in order to support the affected categories of the population in the future. Current information about needs, photo reports of activities can be found in social networks.
