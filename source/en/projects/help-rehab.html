Project’s mission 

Рelp pay for a trip to the Center for a person with diabetes and their companion. The foundation covers most of the costs of patients staying in our Center on its own, and with your support we can collect enough money and provide our citizens with a full course of the health program.

The project objectives:

Teach people with diabetes to live a full life and to explain to them that diabetes is not a sentence, but a way of life. When receiving visitors, the Center pursues the following goals:

  • To teach a person with diabetes to choose the correct dose of insulin in everyday life and during physical activity.
  • Help establish control and balance in the life of a diabetic, which will help reduce the risk of life-threatening side effects of the disease.
  • Psychological rehabilitation: individual therapy, work on group therapy, support of the diabetic community, which will be a psychological support for patients and their relatives.
